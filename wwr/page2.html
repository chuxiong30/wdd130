Camping:

- Limit is 15 people per campsite

- Our campsites are a decent size and can fit up to 6 tents max.

- Firewood is free, feel free to use

- Clean up after your time here

- Shoes on at all times

- Do not eat anything that you find in the woods (we are not responsible)




Zip-Lining:

- Limit is 10 people per group

- If you have a fear of heights, please do not attempt. Once up, the only way down is
zip-linning through the course with everyone (Unless Emergency.)

- If it is your first time, your instructors will go through everything with you and tell you what you need to do.

- Please do not wear hats or loose clothing

- Pictures will be taken and available for you to take home if you'd like. You may choose as many as you'd like,
but one copy of one picture per preson.
